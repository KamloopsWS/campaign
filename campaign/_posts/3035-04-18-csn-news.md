#### Interviewer: 
Reporter Selene Taris, ComStar News Bureau

#### Interviewee: 
Commander Petterson, leader of the Petterson's Pistolleros

#### Date: 
18th of April, 3035

#### Location: 
Planet Grumium, Forward Operating Base

---

#### Interview: 
**Reporter:** Commander Petterson, thank you for taking the time to speak with us amidst the ongoing conflict here on Grumium. Could you start by detailing the current situation your unit and allies are facing against the Cappellan-backed planetary raid?

**Commander Petterson:** Absolutely. The **Petterson's Pistolleros**, our sponsored units, and our allied forces have been holding the line against a well-funded assault by Cappellan and Pirate forces intent on destabilizing Grumium. Our mercenary unit is focused on protecting key building, settlements and infrastructure, leveraging our knowledge of the terrain. The fighting is intense, but our resolve remains unshaken.

**Reporter:** The Cappellan Confederation's involvement has raised many questions. What do you think is the motivation behind their support of this raid?

**Commander Petterson:** It's a classic power play. Grumium holds value in resources and strategic position. The Cappellans want control, plain and simple.

**Reporter:** Given the stakes, what challenges have been the hardest to overcome during this defense?

**Commander Petterson:** The biggest challenge is the enemy’s superior numbers and resources. They’ve brought in advanced BattleMechs and heavy artillery, which strain our defensive positions. They are also working alongside local pirate forces, the remnants of the Ronin and finally the Steiner seperatist leftovers. However, our adaptability and local intelligence have allowed us to counter many of their maneuvers effectively.

**Reporter:** Understandable. Now, if I may, there’s been some troubling stories circulating locally and possibly from Cappellan sources about one of your sponsored units officers, a man named **Kresek**, in the **Crimson Lancers**, people are labeling him as the "Butcher of Grumium." How do you respond to these allegations?

**Commander Petterson:** Those claims are baseless. **Kresek** is a loyal and disciplined officer. The Cappellans use such propaganda to sow distrust and discredit us. It's a tactic to weaken our resolve.

**Reporter (pressing):** Commander, many locals and even some within the military circles accuse **Kresek** of war crimes during previous skirmishes on Grumium. Stories of shooting prisoners, targeting of mostly peaceful civilians, bombarding dense civilian areas, even the destruction of orphanages. Would you not agree that such a moniker as **"Butcher"** might be justified given the evidence?

**Commander Petterson:** I won’t deny that war leaves scars and that some commanders make hard decisions. But labeling someone without full context does no favors to those of us trying to bring stability. I must decline to comment further on **Kresek** specifically.

**Reporter:** Interesting. But there are reports suggesting **Kresek** hires rather... colorful characters. Bloodthirsty samurai like his friend and co-commander **Yamato 'Spectre' Takeda**, ex-ronin paramilitary terrorists like **Jia-Xin 'Mantis' Yang**, he hires notorious arms smuggler and mechwarrior **Rayne 'Zero' Smith** and gets ex-pirates from Buttehold to administrate his logistics, and even drug addicted mechwarrios like this 'Rompy' character who we just heard was recently captured. Doesn’t that raise concerns about the discipline and ethics within the **Crimson Lancers** unit?

**Commander Petterson:** In wartime, you take what you can get. The people **Kresek** brings in are skilled fighters and experts in their fields. Their pasts don’t define their loyalty or effectiveness. We have strict discipline, and every member understands their role. The priority is winning this fight and protecting Grumium.

**Reporter:** Speaking of people hired by **Kresek**, the **Crimson Lancers** have been active in blunting the Cappellan advance. How do you assess their contribution as the man who sponsored this unit?

**Commander Petterson:** The **Crimson Lancers** have shown exemplary courage and tenacity. Their actions have significantly disrupted the Cappellan forces, though it has come at a great cost in pilots and hardware. I commend their sacrifice and professionalism under fire. Their efforts have bought us crucial time to regroup and fortify our defenses.

**Reporter:** Understood, sir. Shifting back, how do you see the future of Grumium if the **Pistolleros** and your allies succeed in repelling this planetary raid?

**Commander Petterson:** If we succeed, Grumium will have a chance to rebuild and regain control of its destiny. The people here deserve peace and the opportunity to thrive without outside interference.

**Reporter:** Thank you for clarifying these points, Commander. Is there anything else you would like to add about the defense of Grumium?

**Commander Petterson:** Only that we will continue to stand firm against any aggression. The bravery of all our mercenaries and allies, including honorable mention to the **Crimson Lancers**, is a testament to our resolve. Grumium’s future depends on our collective strength and vigilance.

**Reporter:** Thank you, Commander Petterson, for your candid answers. We appreciate your dedication and wish you the best defending Grumium.

**Commander Petterson:** Thank you. We’ll do everything to keep this planet safe.
