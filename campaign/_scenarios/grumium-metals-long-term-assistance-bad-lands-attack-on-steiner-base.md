##### Briefing

Intel recovered from Lyran seperatist officers has revealed possible locations of several hostile forward operating bases south of the capital in the Bad Lands. The planetary governor and Grumium Metals CEO are ordering night strikes against all potential targets. We are assigned the task of attacking and destroying one of the seperatist bases. SatCom intel confirms the base if occupied by atleast one patrolling light lance and defended by a perimeter of turrets.
