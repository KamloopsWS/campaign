##### Briefing

Attempt a stealthy approach on the Ronin industrial facility and destroy the objective buildings. Intel reports that a company of Vinson's Vigilantes mercenaries has been contracted for defense of the facility, so maintain cautiion on your approach to the base. First drop the power grid to disable the turrets and then take out the objective buildings. After primary objective completion you may retreat or stay and fight for control of the field of battle for salvage.

##### After-Action Report

**Salvaged (exchange):** Enforcer ENF-4R, Phoenix Hawk PXH-1, Trebuchet TBT-5N, Wolverine WVR-6R mechs and 4 x Vedette Medium Tank, 4 x Manticore Heavy Tank.

~780K in exchange.
