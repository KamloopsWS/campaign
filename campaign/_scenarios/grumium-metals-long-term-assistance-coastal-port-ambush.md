##### Briefing

We were ambushed during our transit to a defense operation at a Grumium naval port facility by the veterans of the "Dread Legion" including the now widowed wife of the ex-CO seeking revenge for the death of their Commanding officer. The "Dread Legion" were operating custom and/or Star League relic/cache mechs. They were using intel leaked to help plan the ambush and also led to  believing that the dead commanding officer was killed in a cowardly manner by a man named Kresek.


##### After-Action Report

**Salvaged (exchange):** 8xmechs and 4xVTOLs.

~1.5 million in exchange.
