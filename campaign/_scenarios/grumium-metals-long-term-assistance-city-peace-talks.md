##### Briefing

A single lance of our mechs are assigned as the Honor Guard of the Peace Talks. All mechs will be initially running in weapons lock mode with sensors reporting any hostile actions. All parties including the planetary governor, mercenary representatives, Comstar,  Cappellans, DCMS, FRR, Lyrans have agreed to attend the talks.



##### After-Action Report

The official story and truth is that as the cappelan delegates pulled up to the conference entrance they detonated their own vehcile... but there is more nefarious rumors going around the city about a certain MechWarrior (The Butcher of Grumium) going weapons free on the car before the explosion. Our initial 4 mechs are attacked by 36 (3 companies) of Cappellans mechs. Our reinforcements did arrive alongside allied mercenaries. 

**Salvaged (exchange):** 36 x mechs.

~3.4 million in exchange.
