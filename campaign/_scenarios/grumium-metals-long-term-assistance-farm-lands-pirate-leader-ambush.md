##### Briefing

A new gang pirates from offworld based on their unrecognized camo pattern have been reported to be raiding one of the richest farming regions west of the capital city. The governor has tasked us with finding and destroying the raiders.

##### After-Action Report

Most of the pirates were destroyed and/or captured but their leader escaped in his customized Solaris King Crab mech.

**Salvaged (exchange):** 6 x mechs.
