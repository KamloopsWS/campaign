##### Briefing

Cappellan funded marauders and saboteurs are running rampant throughout the capital city. The governor has tasked us with reinforcing the defense of an important Mech Factory and with the side objective of covering the withdrawal of the heavily damaged "Cameron's Legion" mercenaries.

##### After-Action Report

Cameron's Legion succesfully withdrew behind our forces covering fire, we saved the mech factory and destroyed all hostile forces.

**Salvaged (exchange):** 8xmechs, and 24xtanks.

~43 million in exchange.
