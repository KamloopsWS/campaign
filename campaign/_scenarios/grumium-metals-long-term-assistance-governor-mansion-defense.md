##### Briefing

With the fighting around the city intensifying, the governor sent his family into hiding a few months ago. Steiner seperatists have discovered this fact recently. One of the last groups of hostile forces within the Bad Lands area that we know of that have not re-located to the area around the capital have been spotted moving in the region by SatCom near the governor's rural estate. You have been ordered to protect the governor's family at all costs and if successfull you will be generously rewarded.
