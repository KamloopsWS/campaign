Overview
This is a refit kit made by Tempest Metal Works. This Hatchetman variant is a specialized addon kit for the "HCT-TMW2" refit kit which replaces the stock engine with the GM 270 engine from the venerable Phoenix Hawk. This allows the 'Sigurd' to chase down and melee most of the Draconis Combines's faster mech designs.
